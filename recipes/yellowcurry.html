Yellow Curry

Yellow Curry is a very popular dish from Thailand. It is a common first dish for people new to Thai food as it is milder when it comes to its spices.

Ingredients

  • Yellow Curry Paste
  • Onions
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Sesame Oik
  • Fish Sauce
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Coconut Milk

Steps for Cooking

  1. Head the sesame oil to medium heat in a pan.
  2. Add the Curry Paste
  3. Sautee the onions,Garlic, and ginger
  4. Add the coconut milk and fish sauce
  5. Finally, add the potates and carrots
  6. Bring to a boil then reduce to low head for 20 minutes, or until potatoes and carrots are tender
