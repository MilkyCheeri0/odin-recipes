Prik Nam Pla

Prik Nam Pla is a very popular spicy sauce from Thailand. It is know for its heat, acidity, and fish sauce. If you dont like fish sauce you can substiture for soy sauce, or something simular.

Ingredients

  • Fish Sauce
  • Thai Chilis
  • Brown Sugar
  • Limes
  • Garlic

    • Steps for making this sauce.

    1. Dice Thai chilis into thin slices and put into a bowl
    2. Add fish sauce.
    3. Mine and add garlic.
    4. Cut and squeeze limes into bowl.
    5. Add brown sugar and mix together.
    6. Let the sauce sit in the fridge for 20 minutes to absorb the flavors.
